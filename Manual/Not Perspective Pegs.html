Everything is different from the pegging of another. Wait, what?

A Not Perspective Pegs module will initially appear with all the pegs sunken into the module. Press any peg to raised the pegs and activate the module.

The pegs will each have five colored faces, all with different colors per peg. Across all the pegs, five of the colored faces will flash.

Each of the flashes will define a position in a 3-digit base-5 values. These values will be lettered A-E in the order that they flash.

  • For the most significant digit, take the 0-indexed position of the peg it flashed on, starting from the top, and going clockwise.
  • For the middle significant digit, take the 0-indexed position of the perspective it flashed from, starting from the top, and going clockwise.
  • For the least significant digit, take the 0-indexed position of the color in the Color Table, from top to bottom.
