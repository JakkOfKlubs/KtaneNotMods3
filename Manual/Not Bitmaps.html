A Not Bitmaps module will cycle through four different 8×8 bitmaps.

Within the bitmaps, a single 5×5 configuration from the tables below is present. Neither the bitmap nor the configuration wrap around.

Based on the configuration, and its position on the bitmap relative to the center, determine which buttons to press while that bitmap is shown.
